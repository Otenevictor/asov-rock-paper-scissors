Game Rules

  1. Choices: Players can choose between three options: "rock", "paper", or "scissors".
  2. Winner Determination:
    • Rock beats Scissors.
    • Scissors beats Paper.
    • Paper beats Rock
    • If both players choose the same option, it's a Draw.
  3. Scoring:
    • Player scores a point if they win a round.
    • Bot scores a point if the bot wins a round.
    • The score for both the player and the bot is stored in localStorage to persist across page refreshes.
  4. play with Bot.
  5. Replay Option: After a round, the player can choose to play again by clicking the replay button.
